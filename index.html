About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Smart Materials, Structures and Systems (SMSS) Lab, Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Kanpur. My research focuses on mechanical metamaterials, solid mechanics, and vibration control systems, with experience across India and the UK.

Research Interests

Mechanical metamaterials, solid mechanics and design, vibration control, piezoelectric energy harvesting, FEA modeling for multiphysics systems, elastic wave physics, and AI-based inverse design.
